Project: LoOp

This project tasked us with creating an app focused on pulse. The assignment was based on the United Nations' Global Goal No. 3: 'Good Health and Well-being; to ensure healthy lives and promote well-being for all at all ages.' I chose to create a social entertainment app with a high pulse, which I named 'LoOp'. The app is downloaded to your phone and synced with your smartwatch, which measures your pulse while you ride amusement park rides, and also serves as support for those with pulse-related health conditions. In the app, you can track your pulse and see which rides gave you the highest and lowest pulse.
